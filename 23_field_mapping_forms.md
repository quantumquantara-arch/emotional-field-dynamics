# Field Mapping Forms
Emotional Field Dynamics (EFD)

These standardized forms help clinicians assess, document, and track emotional fields, vector patterns, thresholds, and trajectories. They may be printed, digitized, or built into electronic health systems.

# Summary

These forms standardize EFD field assessment across clinicians and research settings.  
They support:
- early warning detection  
- field mapping  
- trajectory prediction  
- session planning  
- longitudinal tracking  
- crisis prevention  
- non-coercive practice  

They are designed to be integrated into clinical workflow, digital applications, and training programs.
